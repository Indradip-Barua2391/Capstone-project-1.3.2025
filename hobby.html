my Favourite hobbies

Section one

I have a variety of hobbies that keep me active and engaged. Badminton is a big part of my life – I love the fast pace and challenge of the game, constantly striving to improve. When I’m not on the court, cycling is my go-to activity. It’s a great way to clear my head and explore new places. Gaming also plays a key role in how I unwind, especially with games like GTA 5 and Vigor. Whether it’s on the bike, the court, or in a game, I’m always looking for ways to push myself and enjoy new experiences.
