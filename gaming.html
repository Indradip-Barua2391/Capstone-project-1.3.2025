Ghost of tsushima

Ghost of Tsushima is a 2020 action-adventure game developed by Sucker Punch Productions. Set in feudal Japan during the Mongol invasion of 1274, the game follows Jin Sakai, a samurai warrior who must adopt unconventional tactics to protect his home island of Tsushima. Players can explore a vast open world, engaging in sword combat, archery, and stealth while mastering different combat stances to overcome diverse enemies. The game garnered widespread acclaim for its stunning visuals, immersive atmosphere, and engaging combat system, earning numerous awards and solidifying its place as a fan-favorite title.
